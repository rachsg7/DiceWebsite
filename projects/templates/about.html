About Tressym Dice

Hi! I'm Rachel and I like to make dice for TTRPG games including Dungeons & Dragons and Pathfinder. They are made using epoxy resin, dyes, and whatever fun things I can think of putting inside.

As these are handmade dice, some defects may be present but they will not affect playability. I will provide pictures showing any known defects in the die, and they will be marked down in price accordingly.
